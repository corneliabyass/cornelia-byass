Response – Typeface Design

03 RESPONSE - TYPEFACE DESIGN
An experimental typeface that reacts to sound. The typeface changes weight depending on the heaviness and intensity of the music: The glyphs and music develop together. This project involved typography design, sound design and motion graphics, and aligning the three whilst creating.

Nug Nation – Record Design

04 NUG NATION – RECORD DESIGN

Developing the visual identity for Suckerpunch label. I have worked with producer and DJ Papa Nugs to design records and promote his album releases; creating print, digital and animations to promote his upcoming projects.

MOSS - Hoarding Design

05 Moss - Hoarding Design

Creating large scale print and hoarding design for the re-brand of Moss. My job, as a designer, was to create and communicate a more contemporary feel for the brand.
